Sites:

I buy cubes from The Cubicle I have made around 8 orders from them over the past two and a half years and the items always arrive on time.
Also, using the App on my phone called Shop, I can track where my package is located. I mainly use the Cubicle though for ordering more than 1 item,
which makes it a good site for buying items from when starting out. If you're just buying a singular cube, it's cheaper to buy from Amazon.

Cubes:

The Yuxin Little Magic 3x3. Its my favorite starter cube and actually my main cube I still use today despite having other cubes. I personally magnetized mine and lubricate it once a month and its by far my personal favorite.

Searching for a good cube:

When searching for a cube, look for cubes with:
magnets
good ratings
youtube review videos
