Cubes to Buy and sites:

I buy cubes from The Cubicle I have made around 8 orders from them over the past two and a half years and the items always arrive on time.
Also, using the App on my phone called Shop, I can track where my package is located. I mainly use the Cubicle though for ordering more than 1 item,
which makes it a good site for buying items from when starting out. If you just want to buy a singular cube i personally would recommend amazon and a cube from below: The Yuxin Little Magic 3x3. Its my favorite starter cube and actually my main cube I still use today despite having other cubes. I personally magnetized mine and lubricate it once a month and its by far my personal favorite.
